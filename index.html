loosetime.js

The sensible and easy way to input dates or times on a website.

Download the latest loosetime.js

Datetime pickers are great for when you don't exactly know what Date or Time you are wanting.

However in situations were you know the date e.g. (your date of birth), they can be a real hindrance.

For situations like that, loosetime.js lets you just enter the date or time without having to worry about formatting or validation.

Giving users a much more enjoyable experience completing this information.

Demo§

// 24hour time in HH24:mm:SS
            loosetime("HH24:mm:SS", "element1", "13:03:03");
// Date input in DD/MM/YYYY format
            loosetime("DD/MM/YYYY", "element2", "DD/MM/YYYY");
//Full Date and Time DD/MM/YYYY HH12:mm:SS
            loosetime("DD/MM/YYYY HH12:mm:SS", "element3", "DD/MM/YYYY HH12:mm:SS");
